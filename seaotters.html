        Sea Otter Exhibit at the Monterey Bay Aquarium

        Enjoy the antics of our playful southern sea otters as they romp, tumble and wrestle like their brethren in the bay. Watch them participate in fun enrichment activities with artificial kelp, ice and other toys—it all helps keep them healthy and stimulated!

        The mission of the nonprofit Monterey Bay Aquarium is to inspire conservation of the oceans. Located at the ocean's edge, the Aquarium is your window to marine life, home to sea otters,penguins, sharks, jellies and thousands of other marine animals and plants.
